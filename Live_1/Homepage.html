Fleur épanouie


Company Profile

Fleur épanouie is a manufacturer, distributor and retailer of a luxurious fashion items from hair accesories to a highheels. This company was founded from the inspiration of a housewife, Aeris Le Ciel who thought of providing cheap and quality fashion items for everyone. She first start it from a small business with 10 employee. Now Fleur épanouie have more than 15000 employee with more than 20 branch all around the world.

Fleur épanouie headquarters is located in Paris,France.
